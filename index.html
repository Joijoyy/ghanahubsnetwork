WHAT WE DO

We facilitate access to three critical resources for start-ups: skills, funding and network. We exist in the form of incubators, accelerators, co-working spaces and other innovation spaces.

HUBS

Tech and business hubs are fast growing in Ghana. These are springing across many of the ten (10) regions of Ghana. These hubs are largely run by individuals that are determined to change the Ghanaian narrative on capacity building, project funding, shared office spaces, collaborative projects and creativity.
